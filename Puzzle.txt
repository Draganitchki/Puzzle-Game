"Against the Grain" offers hand-made wooden furniture at reasonable prices. Each item is made by an in-house employee. Using only the clues that follow, match each item to the employee who crafted it, and determine its price and the type of wood used to make it.
Remember, as with all grid-based logic puzzles, no option in any category will ever be used more than once. If you get stuck or run into problems, try the "Clear Errors" button to remove any mistakes that might be present on the grid, or the "Hint" button to see the next logical step in the puzzle.

1. Irma's piece is either the $250 piece or the $275 item.
2. Josephine's piece costs 25 dollars more than the piece made of ash.
3. Gwen's item was made of ash.
4. The item made of chestnut costs less than Gwen's piece.
5. The piece made of elm costs 25 dollars less than Irma's piece.
